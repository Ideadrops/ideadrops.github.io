We are an independent public benefit entity that strives to make the world a better place.

How we work:

An idea

Someone comes to us with an idea that will help the world and if we like it we make it - either for a fee (we have to eat!) or pro-bono (free as in air!).

We do the work

Our small team of wonderful workers will start working on the idea trying to make it come to life and help the world.

Launch!

Once the project is done we help launch it and get it going! (one day we hope we can work on rockets that can actually launch!).
